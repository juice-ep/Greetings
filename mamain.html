🚀 How to Use This MVP

  1. Setup: Choose whether this device is the TV (audio source) or Phone (audio receiver)
  2. TV Setup: If this is your TV, click "Capture Audio" to enable microphone/system audio capture
  3. Phone Setup: If this is your phone, use the connection URL shown to connect to the TV
  4. Test: Use the YouTube video above to test audio streaming quality and latency
  5. Validate: Check if audio streams smoothly from TV to phone over WiFi
